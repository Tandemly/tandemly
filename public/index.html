Why? Innovation is hard.

In order to scale your business you need a shift in mindset. You need to think of yourself as a software business. Increasingly, companies don't ask the right questions about their product and their customers.

Tandem.ly can help you make this shift in mindset. We can help implement and take advantage of a lean product development strategy that starts building the right culture to create innovative products that are extensions of your organization's customer touchpoints and brand.

Making A Shift in Mindset
  • Principles are greater than process
  • Software is a creative endeavor, not an assembly line
  • A culture that embraces continuous change
  • Focus on outcomes, not ouptut
  • Remove silos and integrate teams
  • Sense and respond to the customer
Industrial management tactics don't work.

Unlike producing commodities like toothbrushes, building software is a continuous, unending creative process surrounded by uncertainty about requirements, the market, users and more. Stop managing your software process like an assembly line — you're not making toothbrushes.

Design shouldn't be an afterthought.

Not only is software a creative endeavor, it's a team sport as well. The entire team needs to be involved in product discovery activities like user research and design, not just designers. This builds teams with a shared understanding and empathy for their users.

Focus on culture, not process.

"Culture eats process for breakfast." 1 Organizations need to change their culture and structure to take advantage of the creative, agile approaches to building innovative software. A culture that incentivizes learning and experimentation will generate innovative outcomes.
